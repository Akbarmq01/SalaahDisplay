Fajr = Anas Ibn Maalik (May Allah be pleased with him) reported that the Prophet (May the peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) said there are three qualities, whoever has them, will taste the sweetness of imaan: To love Allah and His messenger (Peace and blessings of Allah be upon him) more than anyone else, to love a slave of Allah only for the sake of Allah and to hate returning to infidelity after Allah has saved him from it as he would hate to be thrown into the fire of hell - (Narrated in Bukhari and Muslim)
