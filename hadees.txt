Fajr = Ibn Abbas Radhiallah anho:Allah's Messenger(SAW) said! Do not observe saum (fasting) before the advent of Ramadan.Observe saum at the sighting of the crescent of Ramadan and terminate it on sighting the crescent of Shawwal.If the sky is overcast,complete the month as of thirty days.(Al-Trimidhi)

Duhr = Oh blessed prophet (SAW)! I do not see you fasting any month more than the month of shaban.Blessed prophet(SAW) replied: People are ignorant of it, being between the months of Rajab and Ramadan all deeds are raised towards rabb ul alameen in that month.Thus, I wish that my deeds are raised when I am fasting.(Nisai,Musnad Ahmad)
